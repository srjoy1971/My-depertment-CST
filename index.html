Sylhet Polytechnic Institute

About Institute

Sylhet Polytechnic Institute is a government technical institute in Bangladesh. It provides diploma engineering education under BTEB. Students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical technical skills to build strong careers in engineering and technology sectors.

Computer Technology Department

The Computer Technology Department is one of the most popular departments of Sylhet Polytechnic Institute. The department offers a 4-year diploma program that prepares students for modern IT industries.

Students learn programming, database management, networking, software development, and web technologies. The department also focuses on practical labs, project work, and industry-related skills to make students ready for real-world careers.

Graduates from this department work in software companies, IT support organizations, freelancing marketplaces, and many pursue higher studies in engineering universities.
